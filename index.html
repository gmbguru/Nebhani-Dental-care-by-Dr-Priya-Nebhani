Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I visit the dentist?

We recommend visiting every 6 months for regular check-ups and cleaning. However, patients with specific conditions may need more frequent visits.

Is root canal treatment painful?

No, modern RCT is painless due to advanced anesthesia and techniques. We ensure complete comfort during the procedure.

How long does teeth whitening last?

Results typically last 6-12 months depending on your lifestyle. We recommend touch-ups annually for best results.

Do you accept dental insurance?

Yes, we work with most major insurance providers. Please contact us or bring your insurance card for verification.

What should I do in case of a dental emergency?

Call us immediately at 9301135287. We prioritize emergency cases and can often accommodate same-day appointments.

Dental Tips

Oral Health Tips

Proper Brushing

Brush twice daily for 2 minutes using fluoride toothpaste. Use gentle circular motions on all surfaces.

✓ 2 minutes × 2 times daily

Flossing Daily

Floss between teeth daily to remove food particles and plaque that brushing cannot reach.

✓ Daily before bedtime

Healthy Diet

Avoid sugary foods & drinks. Eat calcium-rich foods like milk, cheese, and leafy greens.

✓ Limit sugar intake
